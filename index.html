About Alliance Industrial Doors

Alliance Industrial Doors Ltd is a specialist in the manufacture, supply and installation of all types of industrial doors. Based at Sovereign Works in Dudley, West Midlands, we provide services throughout the UK.

Established in 1999, we have built a reputation for excellence. Our team of experienced engineers and manufacturers ensure that every door leaving our factory meets the highest standards.

We are fully certified members of the DHF (Door & Hardware Federation), ensuring compliance with all safety regulations and British Standards.
